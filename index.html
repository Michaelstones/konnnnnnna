Our Story

Born from a Real Nigerian Problem

Finding reliable service professionals in Nigeria has never been easy. Whether you need an electrician, plumber, engineer, technician, or artisan, the process often depends on referrals, social media posts, or word-of-mouth recommendations. Customers are left uncertain about quality, reliability, and accountability — while thousands of skilled professionals struggle to reach people beyond their immediate network.

KonnectU was created to bridge that gap.

We believe finding trusted professionals should be as simple as booking a ride or ordering food. By bringing customers and service providers together on one trusted connection platform, we're making it easier to discover verified professionals, compare profiles, build trust, and create real opportunities for everyone.

We also recognised the unique challenge faced by millions of Nigerians abroad who need trusted professionals to manage projects and properties back home — and built KonnectU to serve them too.

Leadership

The Team Behind KonnectU

Agboola Shonekan
Founder & Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Agboola Shonekan founded KonnectU Solutions Limited with a simple belief: finding a trusted professional should never be a matter of luck.

A Civil Engineer with a background in construction, project management, and technology, Agboola spent years building FixItAll Handyman Multi Services Limited into a respected provider of construction, maintenance, and property services in Nigeria. Throughout that journey, he witnessed firsthand the everyday frustration people faced when trying to find skilled, reliable, and accountable professionals — a problem that was common, costly, and largely unsolved.

After relocating to Canada, that challenge took on an even broader dimension. He saw Nigerians in the diaspora struggling to coordinate repairs, renovations, and property maintenance back home — often sending money across borders with little visibility, no accountability, and even less recourse when things went wrong.

KonnectU was built from that realisation. A trusted digital marketplace where customers can confidently connect with verified artisans, skilled tradespeople, and professionals — while service providers gain the visibility and opportunities they need to grow.

Agboola is also the creator of AdunniTrak, a digital operations platform for the mining, quarrying, and industrial sectors — reflecting his broader passion for using technology to solve real operational challenges across industries.

How Trust Is Built

Our Verification Process

Trust isn't claimed — it's built. Here is exactly how every KonnectU provider earns their place on the platform.

1
Identity Verification
Every service provider registers with verifiable identity information. This creates a baseline of accountability from the very first step — so you always know who is behind a profile.
2
Business Verification
For corporate and business providers, additional business details are collected and reviewed. This helps customers confidently identify professional organisations versus individual practitioners.
3
Credential & Skills Review
Providers build detailed profiles including their skills, service areas, experience, and relevant qualifications. Accurate, honest representation is a condition of listing on KonnectU.
4
Community Feedback
Once active, real customer reviews and ratings become part of every provider's permanent record. The community holds providers accountable — and great providers are rewarded with visibility and trust.
5
Continuous Monitoring
Trust is not a one-time check. KonnectU monitors provider activity, reviews platform interactions, and takes action on any behaviour that falls below the standards our community deserves.
